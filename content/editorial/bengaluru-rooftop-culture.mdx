# The New Bengaluru Rooftop Mood

Bengaluru rooftop culture is no longer only about a view. The best spaces win
through soft lighting, good service, a beautiful table, and the feeling that a
couple can own a small pocket of the city.

This note can grow into neighborhood guides, café edits, stay stories, and brand
collaborations that still feel personal.
